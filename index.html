Our Services

What we are best at - expert lawn & landscaping

Sod Work

Nothing makes for a better yard than new sod to restart the fresh green look of a property. We offer partial yard and full yard sod layouts. We offer a variety of grass types and can help pick the best for your lawn conditions.

Irrigation

For residential and commercial properties we are experts in irrigation installations, repairs and general maintenance. We have not only the tools but expertise required for complex irrigation work.

Full Lawn Services

For residential, commercial, and industrial properties we cover mowing, property maintenance, and more. We know life gets busy, and we are here to help keep your lawn looking fresh.

Hardscaping

We are experts at residential hardscaping including the design and build of retaining walls, patios and flagstone walkways. We don't do some larger hardscape jobs but contact us to learn more.

Skid Steer Work

For residential and commercial properties we offer underbrush clearing with a bush hog, grapple work for debris removal and tree branch clearing, sod layouts and general dirt work.

Stain Work

Whether it is a boat dock, pier, fence, sea wall, deck, gazebo or more we can give it a new finish to look like new or even better than it first looked!

Pressure Washing

For commercial, industrial and residential properties we can remove the layers of dirt and debris that accumulate through expert pressure washing. We utilize soft washing equipment and techniques on your home to ensure a thorough clean without any damages.

Driveway Repairs and Gravel Work

Our Driveway Repairs and Gravel Work service is perfect for homeowners who need their driveway fixed or gravel added. We can help you with any repair or installation needs you may have!

Faqs

Your questions answered

Lawn Care: I can't get my grass to grow given low sunlight - what should I do?

We typically recommend centipede grass (Eremochloa ophiuroides) to grow in the shade. We can provide an expert recommendation once we scope your property.

What is the best way to get a quote?

The best way to get in touch is to call or text (205) 966-3564. We try to provide personal attention to every customer.

Staining: Do you use environmentally safe products or pet/kid friendly?

Yes, we ensure all stains are non toxic and safe for you, your family and pets.

What are your standard hours?

We are open 7:00AM-5:00PM Monday-Friday, and 8:00AM-1:00PM on Saturdays. We are closed on Sundays.

Tree Trimming: For trees and shrubs that interfere with electrical lines is there a safe way to cut them?

Yes, we have special long telescoping cutters for tight areas.

Mowing: Do you have a guard on the mower and do you bag the grass?

We have a shoot blocker to ensure that debris aren’t dangerously ejected out of the mower. We can bag grass if desired.

What type of payments do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (processed through Square) and mobile payments through PayPal, Venmo and CashApp.

Pressure Washing: Do you use direct pressure around windows and doors?

No, we use soft washing technique near the house. These techniques apply very light soap and low pressure to ensure a thorough clean without causing any damage.
